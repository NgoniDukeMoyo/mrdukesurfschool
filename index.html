ABOUT US

Mister Duke Surf School is a mobile surf School that is commited to sharing the love of the ocean and stoked to all. We create a space for you to build confidence, belief and an experience of riding a wave than can not be explained while letting mother nature heal you through her waters. We are with you every step of the way, from teaching you the techniques to riding the waves. With us you can enjoy a relaxed surf atmosphere community and learn everything about surfing under the guidance of experienced and professional surf instructors

Our Community Outreach Efforts

Realising that the ocean has an incredible ability to heal, restore, releave and bring joy, we as an organisation strive to share this with those are not given the opportunity to expewrience this due to disabilities, old age or financially out of reach. We do this to share the beauty of the ocean and its power.
